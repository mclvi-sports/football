## Scenario Ideas

### Revenge Game Scenario
**Trigger:** Player faces former team for first time after trade/FA departure

**Narrative Elements:**
- Pre-game media hype ("X returns to face former team")
- Player interview quotes ("I have nothing to prove" / "They let me go")
- Crowd reaction (boos/cheers depending on departure terms)

**Gameplay Effects:**
| Factor | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Player OVR boost | +5 to +15 based on departure bitterness |
| Motivation | Higher effort, more aggressive plays |
| Risk | Increased chance of mistakes (trying too hard) |
| XP bonus | 2x XP earned if player performs well |

**Departure Types:**
| Type | Bitterness | OVR Boost |
|------|------------|-----------|
| Cut/Released | High | +15 |
| Traded (unwanted) | High | +12 |
| Contract not offered | Medium | +10 |
| Left in FA (money) | Low | +5 |
| Left in FA (winning) | Low | +5 |
| Traded (requested) | None | +0 |

**Post-Game Narratives:**
- "X dominates former team in revenge game"
- "X struggles under pressure of return"
- "Classy performance - X downplays revenge angle"

## Online H2H: My Cards Mode (Separate from Franchise)

### Core Concept
A standalone competitive mode where users collect player cards, build teams, and compete online. Separate progression from Franchise mode.

### Card System

**Card Rarities:**
| Tier | Color | Pull Rate | OVR Range |
|------|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Common | Gray | 50% | 60-69 |
| Uncommon | Green | 25% | 70-74 |
| Rare | Blue | 15% | 75-79 |
| Epic | Purple | 7% | 80-84 |
| Legendary | Gold | 2.5% | 85-89 |
| Ultimate | Red/Holographic | 0.5% | 90-99 |

**Card Types:**
| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Base | Standard player cards |
| Flashback | Past versions of players |
| Rookie Premiere | Hyped rookies |
| Team of the Week | Weekly top performers |
| Legends | Retired greats |
| Limited Edition | Time-limited special cards |
| Signature Series | Player autograph versions |
| Playoff Heroes | Postseason performers |
| Super Bowl Icons | Championship MVPs |

### Pack System

**Pack Types:**
| Pack | Cost | Contents |
|------|------|----------|
| Standard | 500 coins | 5 cards, 1 Uncommon+ guaranteed |
| Pro | 2,500 coins | 7 cards, 1 Rare+ guaranteed |
| Elite | 10,000 coins | 10 cards, 1 Epic+ guaranteed |
| Legend | 25,000 coins | 12 cards, 1 Legendary guaranteed |
| Ultimate | Premium currency | 15 cards, better odds |

**Earning Coins:**
- Win H2H match: 500-1,000 coins
- Daily challenges: 200-500 coins
- Weekly objectives: 2,000-5,000 coins
- Season rewards: 10,000+ coins
- Selling duplicate cards

### Team Building

**Roster Requirements:**
- 22 starters (11 offense, 11 defense)
- Salary cap system (total OVR budget)
- Chemistry bonuses (same team, scheme, division)

**Chemistry Types:**
| Chemistry | Bonus |
|-----------|-------|
| Team (5+ same team) | +2 OVR to matching players |
| Scheme (matching scheme) | +1 OVR, +5% badge activation |
| Division (5+ same division) | +1 chemistry |
| College (3+ same college) | +1 development |

### Competitive Modes

**Head-to-Head Seasons:**
| Division | Wins to Promote | Rewards |
|----------|-----------------|---------|
| Rookie | 4 wins | 5,000 coins |
| Pro | 5 wins | 10,000 coins + pack |
| All-Pro | 6 wins | 20,000 coins + Epic pack |
| All-Madden | 7 wins | 50,000 coins + Legend pack |
| Legend | 8 wins | Ultimate pack + exclusive card |

**Weekend League:**
- 25 games per weekend
- Rewards based on wins
- Top tier: Exclusive Ultimate cards

**Tournaments:**
- Single elimination brackets
- Entry fee (coins)
- Prize pool distribution

### Card Progression

**Training System:**
- Use duplicate cards to train
- +1 OVR per training level (max +5)
- Higher rarity = more training XP

**Power-Up Cards:**
- Upgradeable versions of players
- Requires base card + training
- Unlocks additional abilities

### Marketplace

**Auction House:**
- List cards for sale
- Set buy-now price or auction
- 10% transaction fee
- Search/filter by position, OVR, rarity

**Quick Sell:**
- Instant coins for unwanted cards
- Lower value than auction

### Sets & Collections

**Set Types:**
| Set | Reward |
|-----|--------|
| Team Collection | Team-specific Legend card |
| Position Heroes | 90+ OVR position master |
| Weekly Sets | Team of the Week player |
| Flashback Sets | Past player versions |

### Seasons & Resets

**Season Length:** 3 months

**Season Reset:**
- Competitive ranks reset
- Cards carry over
- New card series released
- Previous series harder to pull

## Player Attribute Re-Roll System

### Core Concept
Allow users to re-roll player attributes after draft/generation, with costs and limits tied to GM skills, coaches, and scouts.

### Re-Roll Types

| Re-Roll | What Changes | Cost |
|---------|--------------|------|
| Single Attribute | One attribute re-randomizes | Low |
| Attribute Category | All attrs in category (Physical, Mental, etc.) | Medium |
| Full Re-Roll | All attributes regenerate | High |
| Trait Re-Roll | Re-roll one trait slot | Very High |
| Potential Re-Roll | Re-roll ceiling/potential | Extreme |

### Cost Currency Options

| Currency | Source |
|----------|--------|
| GM Points | Earned through gameplay |
| Scouting Points | From scout department |
| Training Points | From practices/development |
| Cap Space | Actual salary cap dollars |

### Limits & Restrictions

**Per Player Limits:**
| Player Type | Max Re-Rolls |
|-------------|--------------|
| Draft Pick | 3 total (before signing) |
| Rookie (Year 1) | 2 per season |
| Veteran | 1 per season |
| Free Agent signing | 1 (at signing only) |

**Cooldowns:**
- 1 re-roll per player per week
- Cannot re-roll same attribute twice in a row

### Trait Unlocks

| Trait | Re-Roll Bonus |
|-------|---------------|
| **Moldable** | +2 re-rolls per season |
| **Set in Ways** | Cannot be re-rolled (negative) |
| **Late Bloomer** | Re-rolls more likely to increase |
| **Raw Talent** | First re-roll is free |
| **Perfectionist** | Re-rolls cost 50% less |

### Coach Bonuses

| Coach Skill | Bonus |
|-------------|-------|
| **Player Development** | +1 re-roll per player |
| **Position Specialist** | Free re-roll for that position |
| **Scheme Fit Expert** | Re-roll scheme-related attrs only |
| **Talent Evaluator** | See re-roll outcome preview |

### Scout Bonuses

| Scout Skill | Bonus |
|-------------|-------|
| **Deep Evaluator** | Reveal hidden potential before re-roll |
| **Attribute Specialist** | +10% better re-roll outcomes |
| **Character Scout** | Can re-roll traits (usually locked) |
| **Combine Expert** | Physical re-rolls improved |

### GM Perks

| Perk | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Second Chance** (Tier 1) | 1 free re-roll per draft |
| **Talent Sculptor** (Tier 2) | Re-rolls cost 25% less |
| **Diamond Polisher** (Tier 3) | Re-rolls can exceed original ceiling |

### Re-Roll Mechanics

**Outcome Distribution:**
| Result | Chance |
|--------|--------|
| Worse (-3 to -1) | 25% |
| Same (±0) | 20% |
| Slightly Better (+1 to +2) | 35% |
| Much Better (+3 to +5) | 15% |
| Jackpot (+6 to +10) | 5% |

**Risk/Reward Options:**
| Mode | Risk | Reward Range |
|------|------|--------------|
| Safe | Low variance | ±2 |
| Standard | Normal | ±5 |
| Risky | High variance | -5 to +10 |
| All-In | Extreme | -10 to +15 |

### UI Flow

1. Select player → View attributes
2. Click "Re-Roll" button
3. Choose: Single attr, Category, or Full
4. Select risk level
5. Confirm cost
6. Animation: Attribute values shuffle
7. Reveal new values (highlight changes)
8. Accept or use "Undo" token (if available)
