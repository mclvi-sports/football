# Draft System - User Experience Design

## Overview

The draft is the most engaging feature for many franchise game players. This document outlines the complete user experience from scouting season through rookie camp.

---

## The Draft Journey (Timeline)

| Phase | When | User Activities |
|-------|------|-----------------|
| **Scouting Season** | Weeks 1-18 | Scout prospects, build draft board |
| **NFL Combine** | Week 19 | Watch workouts, see measurables revealed |
| **Pro Days** | Week 20 | Attend school workouts, private visits |
| **Draft Week** | Week 21 | Make picks, execute trades |
| **Rookie Camp** | Week 22 | Evaluate picks, assign development |

---

## Phase 1: Scouting Season

### Prospect Discovery
- **Big Board**: Sortable list of ~275 draft-eligible prospects
- **Filters**: Position, college, projected round, conference
- **Search**: Find prospects by name or school

### Scouting Reports
User invests scouting points to unlock information:

| Report Type | What It Reveals |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **Basic Profile** | Free - Name, position, college, height/weight |
| **Athletic Testing** | 40-time, vertical, broad jump, agility drills |
| **Medical Evaluation** | Injury history, durability concerns |
| **Interview** | Character, football IQ, work ethic, leadership |
| **Background Check** | Off-field concerns, red flags |
| **Private Workout** | Most accurate overall evaluation |

### Prospect Card Display
- **Photo placeholder** with college colors
- **Measurables**: Height, weight, arm length, hand size
- **Athletic grades**: Speed, explosiveness, agility, strength
- **Scouting grade**: A+ to D scale (based on scout accuracy)
- **Potential indicator**: Star / Starter / Backup / Limited
- **Traits revealed**: Work ethic, character, injury risk
- **College stats**: Position-specific career numbers
- **Accolades**: Awards, All-American status, etc.

### Draft Board Management
- **Drag-and-drop** ranking
- **Position tiers**: Group prospects by round projection
- **Notes**: Add personal notes to any prospect
- **Comparisons**: Side-by-side prospect comparison tool
- **"My Guys"**: Star/favorite prospects for quick access

---

## Phase 2: NFL Combine (Week 19)

### Combine Week Experience
- **Day-by-day schedule**: Position groups work out on different days
- **Live results feed**: See drill results as they happen
- **Risers alert**: Prospects who exceeded expectations
- **Fallers alert**: Prospects who disappointed

### Measurables Revealed
All prospects get baseline measurables revealed:
- Height, weight, arm length, hand size, wingspan
- 40-yard dash, vertical jump, broad jump
- 3-cone drill, 20-yard shuttle, bench press

### Combine Storylines
- "Workout Warriors" - Great athletes, questionable tape
- "Medical Red Flags" - Injury concerns surface
- "Interview Impressors" - Teams buzzing about character
- "No-Shows" - Prospects who skip combine

---

## Phase 3: Pro Days (Week 20)

### School Visits
- **Attend Pro Days**: Free scouting at specific schools
- **Focus on school prospects**: Enhanced evaluation accuracy
- **Weather/conditions**: Indoor vs outdoor affects results

### Private Workouts
- **Request visits**: Bring prospects to your facility
- **Highest accuracy**: Best evaluation possible
- **Limited slots**: Can only host 10-15 private workouts
- **Reveals everything**: Full profile unlocked

### Final Evaluations
- **Lock draft board**: Finalize rankings
- **Trade discussions**: AI teams reach out about picks
- **Mock draft updates**: See how experts project the draft

---

## Phase 4: Draft Day (Week 21)

### Draft Room Interface

#### Main View Components
1. **Pick Ticker** (top)
   - Current pick number and team
   - Countdown timer
   - Recent selections scrolling

2. **Draft Board** (left panel)
   - Your ranked prospects
   - Crossed out when selected
   - Color-coded by position

3. **Team Needs** (right panel)
   - Your roster holes
   - Position depth grades
   - Suggested targets

4. **Selection Panel** (center)
   - When on the clock:
     - Top available by your board
     - Best available by position
     - "Make Selection" button
     - "Trade Pick" button

#### Draft Actions

**Making a Pick**
1. Review available prospects
2. Click prospect to see full profile
3. Confirm selection
4. Watch announcement animation
5. See league reaction (reach/steal/value)

**Trading Up**
1. Click "Trade Up"
2. Select target pick
3. See trade value chart
4. AI counter-offers
5. Accept/reject/counter

**Trading Down**
1. Receive trade offers from AI
2. See what teams want to move up
3. Evaluate pick package
4. Accumulate future picks

**Trading Players + Picks**
1. Package players with picks
2. Full trade negotiation interface
3. Salary cap implications shown

#### Between Picks
- **Watch other selections**: See AI team picks
- **Track position runs**: "4 WRs in last 6 picks"
- **Adjust board**: Re-rank based on who's left
- **Field trade calls**: AI teams reach out

### Draft Rounds
- **Round 1**: Prime time, full coverage, dramatic
- **Rounds 2-3**: Day 2, condensed experience
- **Rounds 4-7**: Day 3, faster simulation option

### Draft Day Settings
- **Auto-pick**: Set preferences, let AI draft
- **Simulation speed**: Real-time / Fast / Instant
- **Trade aggressiveness**: Passive / Normal / Active

## Phase 5: Rookie Camp (Week 22)

### Post-Draft Evaluation
- **Draft grades**: Media grades your draft class
- **Comparison to needs**: Did you fill roster holes?
- **Value analysis**: Reaches vs steals

### Rookie Mini-Camp
- **First impressions**: Initial OVR reveal (±3 from true)
- **Camp standouts**: Rookies exceeding expectations
- **Camp struggles**: Rookies underperforming
- **Position battles**: Competition for roster spots

### Development Assignment
- **Training focus**: Assign development priorities
- **Mentor pairing**: Pair rookies with veterans
- **Role projection**: Starter / Rotational / Practice Squad

### True Reveal Timeline
- **Year 1**: See OVR within ±3 points
- **Year 2**: True OVR fully revealed
- **Potential update**: Development trajectory clear

## Prospect Information Hierarchy

### Always Visible (Free)
- Name, position, college
- Height, weight (basic)
- Projected draft round
- College (name only)

### Scouting Required
| Info Level | Unlock Method |
|------------|---------------|
| Athletic measurables | Combine report / Pro Day |
| Injury history | Medical evaluation |
| Character/IQ | Interview |
| True potential | Elite scout + multiple reports |
| Hidden traits | High-quality scouting report |

### Never Fully Known (Until Drafted)
- True OVR (always estimated)
- Development curve
- How they'll perform in your scheme

## College Representation

### School Tiers (Draft Frequency)
| Tier | Examples | Draft Presence |
|------|----------|----------------|
| **Blue Blood** | Alabama, Ohio State, Georgia | 15-20% of picks |
| **Elite** | Texas, Penn State, Oregon | 25-30% of picks |
| **Power 5** | Most P5 schools | 30-35% of picks |
| **Group of 5** | Boise State, UCF | 10-15% of picks |
| **FCS/Small** | NDSU, JMU | 5-10% of picks |

### College Career Display
- **Stats**: 3-4 year career totals
- **Accolades**: Awards, All-American, etc.
- **School history**: Recent NFL draft picks from school
- **Competition level**: Strength of schedule faced

## Scouting Grades System

### Grade Scale
| Grade | Meaning | Projected Round |
|-------|---------|-----------------|
| **A+** | Elite prospect | Top 5 pick |
| **A** | Premium talent | Round 1 |
| **B+** | Quality starter | Round 2 |
| **B** | Solid starter | Round 3 |
| **C+** | Developmental | Rounds 4-5 |
| **C** | Backup potential | Rounds 6-7 |
| **D** | Camp body | UDFA |

### Grade Accuracy
- Grades can be wrong (intentionally)
- Better scouts = more accurate grades
- Some busts, some sleepers
- "Scouted OVR" vs "True OVR" gap

## Trade Value System

### Pick Value Chart
- Round 1 picks: Highest value (1st overall = 3000 pts)
- Value drops exponentially by round
- Future picks worth ~80% of current year

### Trade Examples
| Move Up | Typical Cost |
|---------|--------------|
| Into Top 5 | Multiple 1sts + player |
| Into Top 15 | 1st + 2nd or 3rd |
| Into Round 2 | 2nd + 4th |
| Into Round 4 | 4th + 6th |

## Mobile-First Design Considerations

### Compact Views
- Swipeable prospect cards
- Collapsible draft board sections
- Bottom sheet for prospect details
- Floating action button for "Make Pick"

### Quick Actions
- One-tap to add to draft board
- Swipe to compare prospects
- Long-press for quick scout

### Notifications
- "You're on the clock" push notification
- Trade offer alerts
- Prospect selected alerts (for tracked prospects)

---

## Engagement Hooks

### Drama Elements
- Trade deadline countdowns
- "Steal of the draft" celebrations
- Bust/boom tracking over career
- Draft class retrospectives (Year 3+)

### Social Features
- Share draft results
- Compare drafts with friends
- Community big boards

### Replayability
- Different team = different needs
- Randomized prospect classes each save
- Multiple draft strategies viable
