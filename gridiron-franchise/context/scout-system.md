# Football Franchise Mobile Game - Scout System

## Overview
The **Scout (Scouting Director)** is responsible for evaluating draft prospects, finding hidden talent, and providing accurate player assessments. A good scout can be the difference between drafting a bust or finding a franchise player.

---

## SCOUT ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES

### Primary Responsibilities
- Evaluate draft prospects
- Scout college players
- Assess free agents
- Provide player ratings and projections
- Identify "sleepers" and hidden gems
- Scout opposing teams

### Impact Areas
- Draft board accuracy
- Scouting report quality
- Hidden attribute revelation
- Potential rating visibility
- Bust/boom prediction accuracy
- Late-round gem discovery

---

## SCOUT ATTRIBUTES

### Core Ratings

#### Overall Rating (OVR)
- Scale: 60-99
- Composite of all scouting attributes
- **Tiers:**
  - 90-99: Elite (reveals almost everything)
  - 85-89: Great (very accurate)
  - 80-84: Good (reliable)
  - 75-79: Average (some uncertainty)
  - 70-74: Below Average (less accurate)
  - 60-69: Poor (many mistakes)

#### Talent Evaluation
- Scale: 60-99
- Accuracy of overall player rating assessment
- **Effect:**
  - 95+: Within Â±1 OVR
  - 85-94: Within Â±2 OVR
  - 75-84: Within Â±3 OVR
  - 65-74: Within Â±5 OVR
  - 60-64: Within Â±7 OVR

#### Potential Assessment
- Scale: 60-99
- Accuracy of projecting player ceiling/potential
- **Effect:**
  - 95+: See exact potential rating
  - 85-94: See potential range (Â±5)
  - 75-84: See potential tier (Superstar/Star/Normal)
  - 65-74: See vague potential (High/Medium/Low)
  - 60-64: Potential completely hidden

#### Trait Recognition
- Scale: 60-99
- Ability to identify player traits/personality
- **Effect:**
  - 95+: Reveal all traits
  - 85-94: Reveal 3-4 traits
  - 75-84: Reveal 2-3 traits
  - 65-74: Reveal 1-2 traits
  - 60-64: Reveal 0-1 traits

#### Bust Detection
- Scale: 60-99
- Identify players likely to underperform
- **Effect:**
  - 95+: 90% accurate bust prediction
  - 85-94: 75% accurate bust prediction
  - 75-84: 60% accurate bust prediction
  - 65-74: 40% accurate bust prediction
  - 60-64: 25% accurate bust prediction

#### Sleeper Discovery
- Scale: 60-99
- Find late-round gems and hidden talent
- **Effect:**
  - 95+: Reveals 5+ sleepers per draft
  - 85-94: Reveals 3-4 sleepers per draft
  - 75-84: Reveals 2-3 sleepers per draft
  - 65-74: Reveals 1-2 sleepers per draft
  - 60-64: Reveals 0-1 sleepers per draft

#### Work Ethic
- Scale: 60-99
- How thoroughly scout evaluates players
- **Effect:**
  - 95+: Can scout entire draft class
  - 85-94: Can scout 75% of draft class
  - 75-84: Can scout 50% of draft class
  - 65-74: Can scout 35% of draft class
  - 60-64: Can scout 25% of draft class

## SCOUT SPECIALIZATIONS

### Position Expertise

Each scout can have expertise in specific position groups:

#### Offensive Specialist
- **Expertise:** QB, RB, WR, TE, OL
- **Bonus:** +10 to all scouting attributes for offensive players
- **Penalty:** -5 to all scouting attributes for defensive players

#### Defensive Specialist
- **Expertise:** DL, LB, DB
- **Bonus:** +10 to all scouting attributes for defensive players
- **Penalty:** -5 to all scouting attributes for offensive players

#### Special Teams Specialist
- **Expertise:** K, P
- **Bonus:** +15 to all scouting attributes for specialists
- **Penalty:** -5 to all other positions

#### Generalist
- **Expertise:** All positions
- **Bonus:** No bonuses
- **Penalty:** No penalties
- **Benefit:** Balanced across all positions

### Regional Expertise

#### East Coast Scout
- **Region:** Northeast, Southeast colleges
- **Bonus:** +10 scouting in region, +5% sleeper discovery
- **Schools:** ACC, Big Ten (East), SEC (East)

#### West Coast Scout
- **Region:** West, Southwest colleges
- **Bonus:** +10 scouting in region, +5% sleeper discovery
- **Schools:** Pac-12, Mountain West, Big 12 (West)

#### Midwest Scout
- **Region:** Midwest, Central colleges
- **Bonus:** +10 scouting in region, +5% sleeper discovery
- **Schools:** Big Ten (West), Big 12 (Central)

#### South Scout
- **Region:** Southern colleges
- **Bonus:** +10 scouting in region, +5% sleeper discovery
- **Schools:** SEC (West), ACC (South), Conference USA

#### National Scout
- **Region:** All regions
- **Bonus:** No regional bonuses
- **Penalty:** No penalties
- **Benefit:** Consistent nationwide

## SCOUTING BUDGET & ALLOCATION

### Budget System

#### Weekly Scouting Points
- Each scout generates scouting points per week
- Points determined by scout OVR and Work Ethic
- **Formula:** Base 100 + (Work Ethic rating Ã— 2)
- Example: 85 Work Ethic = 100 + 170 = 270 points/week

#### Point Costs
- **Top Prospect (Rounds 1-2):** 50 points
- **Mid Prospect (Rounds 3-4):** 30 points
- **Late Prospect (Rounds 5-7):** 15 points
- **Free Agent:** 25 points
- **Full Draft Class:** 2,000 points (requires multiple weeks)

#### Strategic Allocation
- Focus on top prospects for high picks
- Scout depth for late-round gems
- Balance between quantity and quality

---

## SCOUTING PROCESS

### Draft Scouting Timeline

#### Pre-Season (Weeks 1-8)
- **Early Scouting:** First look at top prospects
- **Limited Info:** Basic measurements, college stats
- **Cost:** 25% reduced point cost

#### Mid-Season (Weeks 9-14)
- **Prime Scouting:** Most prospects available
- **Better Info:** Game film, traits starting to reveal
- **Cost:** Normal point cost

#### Late Season (Weeks 15-18)
- **Final Evaluations:** Last chance before draft
- **Complete Info:** Full reports available
- **Cost:** 25% increased point cost (high demand)

#### Combine (Week 19)
- **Physical Testing:** Speed, Strength, Agility revealed
- **Interviews:** Some traits revealed automatically
- **Free Info:** All prospects get basic combine data

#### Pro Day (Week 20)
- **Position Drills:** Position-specific attributes tested
- **Free Info:** More refined measurements

#### Draft Day (Week 21)
- **Final Board:** All scouting complete
- **Make Picks:** Use accumulated information

## SCOUT CONTRACTS & HIRING

### Contract Structure
- **Length:** 1-3 years
- **Salary:** $200K - $2M per year
- **Performance Bonuses:** Successful draft picks

### Hiring Process
1. Browse available scouts
2. View scout ratings, expertise, perks
3. Make offer (salary, years)
4. Scout accepts/rejects

### Firing Scouts
- Can fire at any time
- No buyout (lower-level staff)
- Lose all scouting progress on current prospects

---

## SCOUT PROGRESSION

### Scout XP System
- Scouts earn XP from successful evaluations
- XP spent to upgrade attributes and perks

### XP Gain
- **Correctly Grade Prospect:** 50 XP (within Â±2 OVR)
- **Find Hidden Gem:** 200 XP (late pick becomes star)
- **Avoid Bust:** 150 XP (flag player who busts elsewhere)
- **Draft Hit (Top 3 Rounds):** 300 XP
- **Draft Hit (Rounds 4-7):** 500 XP (harder to predict)

### Attribute Upgrades
- Cost: 500 XP per +1 attribute point
- Max: Can't exceed 99 in any attribute

### Perk Unlocks
- Tier 1: 1,000 XP
- Tier 2: 3,000 XP
- Tier 3: 7,000 XP

---

## SCOUT RECOMMENDATIONS

### Draft Board Assistant

#### Top Prospects Alert
- Scout flags top 5 players at each position
- Based on team needs
- Shows best value picks

#### Sleeper List
- Scout provides list of hidden gems
- Usually Rounds 4-7
- High potential, low current rating

#### Bust Warnings
- Scout flags risky prospects
- Red flag indicators (character, injury, overrated)
- Helps avoid draft mistakes

#### Best Available
- Real-time "best player available" suggestion
- Updates each pick
- Considers team needs + value

---

## SCOUTING STRATEGIES

### For User/GM

**Early Draft Pick (Top 10):**
- Scout top 20 prospects thoroughly
- Focus on elite talent
- Use scout to compare top players

**Mid Draft Pick (11-20):**
- Scout top 50 prospects
- Look for value and scheme fit
- Scout can reveal sleepers in this range

**Late Draft Pick (21-32):**
- Scout top 75 prospects
- Look for fallers and sleepers
- Scout's sleeper discovery crucial

**Multiple Picks:**
- Scout entire draft class
- Build board with tiers
- Scout helps find BPA at each pick

**Trading Up:**
- Scout reveals which prospects worth trading up for
- Bust detection prevents costly trades
- Potential assessment shows future value

- Understands GM's preferences and needs

### Multiple Scouts (Future)
- Hire 2-3 scouts with different specializations
- Combine reports for better accuracy
- Regional scouts cover more ground
- More expensive but more thorough

## BALANCE CONSIDERATIONS

### Scout Impact
- Elite scout can find 2-3 extra starters per draft
- Average scout still provides value (better than guessing)
- Poor scout can lead to busts and wasted picks
- Scout is important but not game-breaking

### Cost vs Benefit
- Elite scout costs $1.5M-$2M per year
- Can save $10M+ by avoiding bust contracts
- Worth the investment for draft-focused teams

### Progression Balance
- Takes 3-4 successful drafts to max out scout
- Elite perks feel transformative
- Mistakes still possible even with great scout

### Design Philosophy
- Scouting adds strategic depth to drafting
- Reduces RNG frustration (know what you're getting)

### Realism vs Gameplay
- Real NFL scouts aren't perfect (neither are ours)
- Even elite scouts miss sometimes (adds drama)
- Hidden information creates tension
- Revelation is satisfying
