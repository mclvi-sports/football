## TRAIT ACQUISITION

### How Players Get Traits

**At Draft/Creation:**
- Players spawn with 1-3 random traits
- Weighted by rarity (more common traits = higher chance)
- Some traits based on background (college, personality tests)

**During Career:**
- **Earned Traits:** Unlock through performance
  - Example: 3 game-winning drives = "Ice in Veins"
  - Example: 50 games without injury = "Iron Man"
  - Example: 5 years with team = "Loyal"

- **Lost Traits:** Can lose traits through actions
  - Example: Multiple injuries = lose "Durable", gain "Injury Prone"
  - Example: Off-field incidents = lose "High Character", gain "Character Concerns"

- **Trait Evolution:** Traits can change over career
  - Example: Young "Quiet" player becomes "Vocal Leader" after 5 years
  - Example: "Money Motivated" player becomes "Ring Chaser" at age 32

---

## TRAIT LIMITS & STACKING

### Limits Per Player
- **Minimum:** 1 trait
- **Maximum:** 5 traits
- **Average:** 2-3 traits per player

### Stacking Rules
- **Cannot Stack:** Conflicting traits (listed in each trait)
- **Can Stack:** Complementary traits (Gym Rat + Film Junkie)
- **Synergies:** Some trait combos unlock bonuses
  - "Gym Rat" + "Focused" = +25% bonus XP (on top of individual bonuses)
  - "Ice in Veins" + "Clutch" = +5 extra attributes in final minute

---

## TRAIT RARITY BREAKDOWN

### Rarity Distribution
- **Common (20-25%):** Most players have at least one
- **Uncommon (8-15%):** Solid traits, fairly available
- **Rare (4-8%):** Special traits, meaningful impact
- **Very Rare (1-3%):** Elite traits, game-changers

### Trait Value by Rarity
- Common traits: +/- 5-10% impact
- Uncommon traits: +/- 10-20% impact
- Rare traits: +/- 20-40% impact
- Very Rare traits: +/- 40%+ impact, unique effects

- Traits affect demands ("Money Motivated" = +30%)

### Team Chemistry
- Positive traits boost chemistry
- Negative traits hurt chemistry
- Balance roster personalities

### Player Development
- Traits determine XP growth rates
- Affect peak age and decline
- Impact long-term value

### Game Simulation
- Traits affect in-game performance
- Clutch traits matter in close games

## TRAIT MANAGEMENT STRATEGIES

### For User/GM

**Building a Team:**
- Mix of personalities (leaders + workers + closers)
- Avoid too many "Divas" or "Locker Room Cancers"
- Value "Gym Rats" for long-term development

**Draft Strategy:**
- Target "Gym Rat" + "Early Bloomer" for quick impact
- Target "Late Bloomer" for long-term value
- Avoid "Lazy" + "Injury Prone" combination

**Contract Decisions:**
- Re-sign "Loyal" and "Team First" players first
- Let "Money Motivated" + "Mercenary" walk in free agency
- Extend "Ring Chasers" on cheap deals late in career

**Roster Balance:**
- Need at least 2-3 "Vocal Leaders" on team
- Limit "Hot Heads" and "Undisciplined" players
- Prioritize "Ice in Veins" for QB/Kicker

## ADVANCED FEATURES

### Trait Mentorship
- Veterans with positive traits mentor rookies
- 25% chance rookie adopts mentor's trait after 2 seasons
- Example: Rookie QB learns "Film Junkie" from veteran QB

### Trait Conflicts
- Negative traits can cause locker room drama
- "Diva" + "Diva" = chemistry disaster
- Manage personalities to avoid implosions

### Trait-Based Events
- Dynamic events triggered by traits
  - "Hot Head" gets ejected from big game
  - "Holdout Risk" demands new contract mid-season
  - "Locker Room Cancer" causes teammate to request trade

### Trait Achievements
- Unlock achievements for collecting rare traits
- "Dream Team" = 5+ players with "Ice in Veins"
- "Character Team" = All players with "High Character"

## NOTES

### Balance Considerations
- Positive traits slightly more common than negative
- Super impactful traits (Iron Man, Ice in Veins) very rare
- Most players have neutral-to-slightly-positive traits
- Negative traits should have some upside (Hot Head = +2 Strength)

### Realism vs. Fun
- Traits create stories and tough decisions
- Some traits feel "unfair" but create engagement
- Balance game impact with narrative value

### Future Expansion
- Add trait evolution system (traits change over career)
- Add trait combinations (unlock special combos)
- Add team culture traits (affect all players)
- Add coach traits (affect players differently)
