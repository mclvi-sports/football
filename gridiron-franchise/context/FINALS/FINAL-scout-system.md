# Scout System

## Overview

The **Scouting Department** is responsible for evaluating draft prospects, finding hidden talent, and providing accurate player assessments. A good scouting staff can be the difference between drafting a bust or finding a franchise player. Teams can employ multiple scouts with different specializations. Scout salaries come from the Owner Budget.

# PART 1: SCOUTING DEPARTMENT STRUCTURE

## Department Composition

Teams can hire **1-5 scouts** with different roles:

| Role | Required | Purpose |
|------|----------|---------|
| Scouting Director | Yes (1) | Lead scout, best attributes |
| Area Scout | Optional (0-2) | Regional expertise |
| Pro Scout | Optional (0-1) | Free agent evaluation |
| National Scout | Optional (0-1) | Generalist backup |

### Department Size by Budget

| Budget Level | Scouts | Annual Cost |
|--------------|--------|-------------|
| Elite | 5 scouts | $4-6M |
| Good | 3-4 scouts | $2.5-4M |
| Average | 2-3 scouts | $1.5-2.5M |
| Below Average | 1-2 scouts | $800K-1.5M |
| Minimum | 1 scout | $400-800K |

---

## Scout Roles

### Scouting Director

- **Primary Role:** Lead all scouting operations
- **Focus:** Draft prospects, final evaluations
- **Bonus:** +5 to all department scouting
- **Required:** Every team must have one

### Area Scout

- **Primary Role:** Cover specific college region
- **Focus:** Deep knowledge of regional prospects
- **Bonus:** +15 in assigned region
- **Limit:** Maximum 2 per team

### Pro Scout

- **Primary Role:** Evaluate NFL free agents and trade targets
- **Focus:** Veteran player assessment
- **Bonus:** +20 to free agent scouting
- **Limit:** Maximum 1 per team

### National Scout

- **Primary Role:** Generalist coverage
- **Focus:** Fill gaps, backup evaluations
- **Bonus:** None (no penalties either)
- **Limit:** Maximum 1 per team

# PART 7: SCOUT GENERATION

## Generating a Scout

```
function generateScout(role, targetOVR):
    scout = {
        role: role,
        age: generateAge(targetOVR),
        experience: calculateExperience(age),
        
        // Generate attributes with variance
        attributes: {
            talentEvaluation: targetOVR + random(-6, 6),
            potentialAssessment: targetOVR + random(-6, 6),
            traitRecognition: targetOVR + random(-6, 6),
            bustDetection: targetOVR + random(-6, 6),
            sleeperDiscovery: targetOVR + random(-6, 6),
            workEthic: targetOVR + random(-6, 6)
        },
        
        // Clamp all to 60-99
        ovr: calculateOVR(attributes),
        
        // Specializations
        positionExpertise: pickExpertise(),
        regionalExpertise: pickRegion(role),
        
        // Perks based on OVR and experience
        perks: generatePerks(targetOVR, experience),
        
        // Contract expectations
        salaryDemand: calculateSalary(role, ovr),
        contractYears: random(1, 3)
    }
    
    return scout
```

# PART 8: SCOUT MARKET & HIRING

## Available Scout Pool

At any time, the market contains:

| Role | Available | OVR Distribution |
|------|-----------|------------------|
| Scouting Directors | 10-15 | 2 Elite, 3 Great, 5 Good, rest Average |
| Area Scouts | 20-30 | 3 Elite, 6 Great, 10 Good, rest below |
| Pro Scouts | 8-12 | 2 Elite, 3 Great, 4 Good, rest below |
| National Scouts | 10-15 | 2 Great, 4 Good, rest Average |

### Pool Refresh

- Pool refreshes annually during offseason
- Fired scouts enter pool
- Retiring scouts leave pool
- New scouts generated to fill gaps

---

## Hiring Competition

### Scout Interest Factors

| Factor | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Salary Offer | 40% |
| Team Success | 20% |
| Facilities | 15% |
| Job Security | 15% |
| Location | 10% |

### Competing Offers

- Multiple teams can pursue same scout
- Higher interest score wins
- Elite scouts may have 2-3 competing offers

---

## Firing & Contracts

| Action | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Fire mid-contract | No buyout (lower staff) |
| Fire during draft prep | Lose all scouting progress |
| Contract expires | Scout enters free market |
| Don't renew | No penalty |
