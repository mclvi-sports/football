# Season Calendar System

## Overview

The Season Calendar System defines the complete flow of a football franchise year, from offseason activities through playoffs. The calendar drives all game events, deadlines, and progression timing.

# PART 2: OFFSEASON PHASE (Weeks 1-12)

## Offseason Sub-Phases

| Sub-Phase | Weeks | Key Activities |
|-----------|-------|----------------|
| Season End | Week 1 | Awards, coach firings, exit interviews |
| Early Offseason | Weeks 2-4 | Franchise tags, coaching hires, facility planning |
| Free Agency | Weeks 5-8 | Signing period, market activity |
| Pre-Draft | Weeks 9-11 | Pro days, workouts, scouting finalization |
| Draft | Week 12 | 7-round draft event |

---

## Week 1: Season End

### Events
| Event | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Championship Game | Final game of previous season |
| Season Awards | MVP, OPOY, DPOY, ROTY, CPOY, Coach of Year |
| All-Pro Selections | 1st Team, 2nd Team announcements |
| Pro Bowl | Exhibition game (simulated) |
| Coach Firings | Underperforming coaches let go |
| Exit Interviews | Player morale/chemistry assessment |

### Player Activities
- Season XP finalized
- Trait evolution checked
- Badge progress locked
- Contract year players enter free agency pool

---

## Week 2: Franchise Tag Window Opens

### Events
| Event | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Franchise Tag Window | Designate 1 player with franchise tag |
| Transition Tag Window | Designate 1 player with transition tag |
| Option Decisions | 5th-year options on 1st round picks |
| Coaching Search | Interview and hire new coaches |

### Tag Rules

| Tag Type | Cost | Player Rights |
|----------|------|---------------|
| Franchise Tag | Top 5 salary at position | Cannot negotiate with other teams |
| Transition Tag | Top 10 salary at position | Can negotiate, original team can match |

---

## Week 3: Coaching Hires & Facility Planning

### Events
| Event | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Coaching Hires Deadline | All HC/OC/DC/ST positions filled |
| Facility Upgrades Begin | Construction starts (takes 1-2 offseasons) |
| Scheme Announcements | New coaches declare offensive/defensive schemes |
| Staff Retention | Coordinator poaching window |

### Facility Decisions
- Review current facility ratings
- Select upgrades (if budget allows)
- Construction timeline set

---

## Week 4: Pre-Free Agency

### Events
| Event | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Cap Space Calculation | Final cap number confirmed |
| Restructure Window | Restructure existing contracts |
| Cuts for Cap | Release players to create space |
| Free Agent Rankings | Scout department ranks available FAs |

### Cap Preparation
```
Available Cap = Salary Cap - Current Commitments + Restructure Savings - Dead Money
```

## Weeks 5-8: Free Agency Period

### Week 5: Legal Tampering & Frenzy

| Day | Event |
|-----|-------|
| Day 1-2 | Legal Tampering (negotiate, no signing) |
| Day 3 | Free Agency Opens (signing begins) |
| Day 3-7 | FA Frenzy (highest activity) |

### Weeks 6-8: Ongoing Free Agency

| Activity | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Signings | Continue signing available players |
| Market Adjustment | Prices drop as pool shrinks |
| Bargain Hunting | Find value deals |
| UDFA Signings | Undrafted free agents from draft |

### Free Agency Market Phases

| Phase | Timing | Market State |
|-------|--------|--------------|
| Frenzy | Days 1-7 | 150% of market value |
| Active | Weeks 2-3 | 100% of market value |
| Slow | Week 4 | 75% of market value |
| Bargain | Post-Draft | 50% of market value |

# PART 5: REGULAR SEASON (Weeks 19-36)

## Season Structure

| Element | Count |
|---------|-------|
| Games per Team | 17 |
| Weeks | 18 |
| Bye Weeks | 1 per team |
| Division Games | 6 (2 per opponent) |
| Conference Games | 4 |
| Inter-Conference | 4 |
| Rotating Division | 3 |

---

## Weekly Schedule Structure

### Standard Week (Non-Bye)

| Day | Activity |
|-----|----------|
| Monday | Recovery, film review, injury treatment |
| Tuesday | Day off (players) / Game planning (coaches) |
| Wednesday | Practice - Install game plan |
| Thursday | Practice - Refine, situational |
| Friday | Practice - Walkthrough, final prep |
| Saturday | Travel, meetings, light walkthrough |
| Sunday | **GAME DAY** |

### Bye Week

| Day | Activity |
|-----|----------|
| All Week | Rest, recovery, minor injuries heal |
| Development | +50% XP bonus applied |
| Scouting | Extra scouting assignments |
| Facility Use | Training facility bonuses apply |

---

## Game Day Schedule

| Time Slot | Games | Designation |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Early | 8-10 games | Standard |
| Late | 3-4 games | Featured |
| Sunday Night | 1 game | Prime Time |
| Monday Night | 1 game | Prime Time |
| Thursday Night | 1 game | Prime Time |

### Prime Time Benefits
- Higher fan engagement
- Badge activation (Prime Time badges)
- Increased revenue
- Greater morale impact

# PART 6: PLAYOFFS (Weeks 37-40)

## Playoff Structure

| Round | Week | Games | Home Team |
|-------|------|-------|-----------|
| Wild Card | Week 37 | 6 | Seeds 1-4 (division winners) |
| Divisional | Week 38 | 4 | Seeds 1-2 |
| Conference | Week 39 | 2 | Higher seed |
| Championship | Week 40 | 1 | Neutral site |

---

## Wild Card Round (Week 37)

### Matchups
| Matchup | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| #2 vs #7 | 2 seed hosts 7 seed |
| #3 vs #6 | 3 seed hosts 6 seed |
| #4 vs #5 | 4 seed hosts 5 seed |

### #1 Seed Advantage
- First round bye
- Home field through conference championship
- Extra week of rest/preparation

---

## Divisional Round (Week 38)

### Matchups
| Matchup | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| #1 vs Lowest Seed | 1 seed plays lowest remaining |
| #2 vs Highest Seed | 2 seed plays highest remaining |

### Reseeding
- Teams reseeded after Wild Card
- Higher seed always hosts

---

## Conference Championship (Week 39)

### Matchups
| Matchup | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| AFC Championship | Top 2 remaining AFC teams |
| NFC Championship | Top 2 remaining NFC teams |

### Stakes
- Winner advances to Championship
- Conference Champion trophy
- Home field to higher seed

## Tiebreaker Procedures

### Two Teams Tied (Division)

| Order | Tiebreaker |
|-------|------------|
| 1 | Head-to-head |
| 2 | Division record |
| 3 | Common games |
| 4 | Conference record |
| 5 | Strength of victory |
| 6 | Strength of schedule |
| 7 | Point differential (conference) |
| 8 | Point differential (all games) |
| 9 | Coin flip |

### Two Teams Tied (Wild Card)

| Order | Tiebreaker |
|-------|------------|
| 1 | Head-to-head (if played) |
| 2 | Conference record |
| 3 | Common games (min 4) |
| 4 | Strength of victory |
| 5 | Strength of schedule |
| 6 | Point differential (conference) |
| 7 | Point differential (all games) |
| 8 | Coin flip |

### Three+ Teams Tied

- Apply tiebreakers to all teams
- Once one team eliminated, restart at step 1
- Continue until all ties broken
