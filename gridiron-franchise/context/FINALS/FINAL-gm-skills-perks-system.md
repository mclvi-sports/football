# GM Skills & Perks System

## Overview

The user plays as the General Manager (GM) of their chosen team. The GM has a **Background** and **Archetype** that provide starting bonuses, plus unlockable **Skills/Perks** that provide buffs across franchise management. GM Points are earned through achievements and spent on skills.

# PART 1: GM CREATION

## Step 1: Choose Background

Your background provides permanent passive bonuses based on your GM's career history.

| Background | Description | Passive Bonus |
|------------|-------------|---------------|
| **Scout** | Former scouting director | +10% scouting accuracy, +1 sleeper/draft |
| **Cap Analyst** | Salary cap expert | +$5M cap space, -5% contract demands |
| **Coach** | Former coordinator/HC | +10% player development, +5 coach appeal |
| **Agent** | Former player agent | -10% contract demands, +10% FA appeal |
| **Analytics** | Data-driven executive | +5% all evaluation accuracy, advanced stats |
| **Legacy** | Family football dynasty | +15% fan loyalty, +10% owner patience |

---

## Step 2: Choose Archetype

Your archetype defines your management style and provides starting skills.

| Archetype | Philosophy | Starting Skill | Synergy Bonus |
|-----------|------------|----------------|---------------|
| **The Builder** | Draft and develop | Training Boost (Bronze) | +25% XP under 25 |
| **The Closer** | Win now, big moves | Trade Master (Bronze) | +15% trade acceptance |
| **The Economist** | Cap efficiency | Salary Cap Wizard (Bronze) | +$3M cap space |
| **The Talent Scout** | Find hidden gems | Hidden Gem (Bronze) | +2 sleepers/draft |
| **The Culture Builder** | Team chemistry | Morale Master (Bronze) | +15% morale floor |
| **The Innovator** | Analytics edge | Inside Sources (Bronze) | Opponent tendencies |

---

## Synergy Bonuses

When Background and Archetype align, you get a synergy bonus:

| Background | Best Archetype | Synergy Bonus |
|------------|----------------|---------------|
| Scout | Talent Scout | +3 sleepers/draft, exact OVR Round 1 |
| Cap Analyst | Economist | +$8M total cap, -10% demands |
| Coach | Builder | +40% young player development |
| Agent | Closer | -15% demands, +25% trade acceptance |
| Analytics | Innovator | Full opponent scouting, advanced projections |
| Legacy | Culture Builder | +25% morale, +20% fan loyalty |

- Change equipped skills **between seasons only**
- Most skills: Only 1 per type equipped
- Some skills stack (Position Guru for different positions)
- Unequipping keeps skill in library
