# Badge System

## Overview

Badges are **situational bonuses** that activate under specific conditions. Players earn badges through **XP investment** or **performance milestones**. Badges provide temporary attribute boosts when their conditions are met, creating meaningful build diversity and progression paths.

# PART 2: BADGE ACQUISITION

## XP-Based Badges (Purchasable)

- Cost XP points to unlock
- Upgrade through tiers (Bronze ‚Üí Silver ‚Üí Gold ‚Üí HoF)
- Player choice on badge investments
- Permanent once purchased

---

## Performance-Based Badges (Earned)

- Automatically awarded for achievements
- Cannot be purchased with XP
- More prestigious than XP badges
- Some badges can be earned OR purchased

---

## XP Earning Rate

| Achievement | XP Earned |
|-------------|-----------|
| Game played | 50 XP |

| Pro Bowl selection | 500 XP |
| All-Pro selection | 750 XP |

| Playoff game | 100 XP |

| MVP award | 1,000 XP |

**Average XP per season:** 1,500-3,000 (starter), 500-1,000 (backup)
