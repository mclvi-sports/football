# Football Franchise Mobile Game - Coaching Staff System

## Overview
The coaching staff consists of **Head Coach (HC)**, **Offensive Coordinator (OC)**, **Defensive Coordinator (DC)**, and **Special Teams Coordinator (ST)**. Each coach has ratings, schemes, perks, and impacts team performance, player development, and game outcomes.

---

## COACH TYPES & ROLES

### HEAD COACH (HC)
**Primary Responsibilities:**
- Overall team leadership and strategy
- Game management (challenges, timeouts, clock management)
- Team morale and culture
- Player discipline
- Affects entire roster

**Impact Areas:**
- Team chemistry/morale
- In-game decision making
- Player development (small boost to all)
- Challenge success rate
- Timeout management

---

### OFFENSIVE COORDINATOR (OC)
**Primary Responsibilities:**
- Offensive game planning and play calling
- Offensive player development
- Offensive scheme implementation

**Impact Areas:**
- Offensive player attribute boosts
- Offensive player development speed
- Offensive play success rate
- QB development (major focus)

---

### DEFENSIVE COORDINATOR (DC)
**Primary Responsibilities:**
- Defensive game planning and play calling
- Defensive player development
- Defensive scheme implementation

**Impact Areas:**
- Defensive player attribute boosts
- Defensive player development speed
- Defensive play success rate
- Turnover generation

---

### SPECIAL TEAMS COORDINATOR (ST)
**Primary Responsibilities:**
- Special teams units and strategy
- Kicker/Punter development
- Return game coordination

**Impact Areas:**
- Kicker/Punter performance
- Return game effectiveness
- Special teams player development
- Field position advantage

---

## COACH ATTRIBUTES

### Core Ratings (All Coaches)

#### Overall Rating (OVR)
- Scale: 60-99
- Composite of all attributes
- Tiers:
  - 90-99: Elite
  - 85-89: Great
  - 80-84: Good
  - 75-79: Average
  - 70-74: Below Average
  - 60-69: Poor

#### Scheme Knowledge
- **Offensive Knowledge (OC, HC):** 60-99
- **Defensive Knowledge (DC, HC):** 60-99
- **Special Teams Knowledge (ST, HC):** 60-99
- Higher = better execution of schemes

#### Player Development
- Scale: 60-99
- Speed of XP gain for players under this coach
- Effect: +5% to +50% XP boost based on rating

#### Motivation
- Scale: 60-99
- Impact on team morale and player performance
- Effect: +2% to +15% team chemistry based on rating

#### Game Planning
- Scale: 60-99
- Quality of game plan preparation
- Effect: +1 to +5 OVR bonus to players in games

#### Adaptability
- Scale: 60-99
- Adjust to opponent's strategy mid-game
- Effect: Better 2nd half adjustments

## COACHING SCHEMES

### Offensive Schemes (OC)

#### West Coast
- **Philosophy:** Short, timing-based passing
- **Best For:** High accuracy QBs, possession WRs
- **Bonuses:** +3 Short Accuracy, +2 Short Route Running
- **Penalties:** -2 Deep Accuracy

#### Spread Offense
- **Philosophy:** Space the defense, fast tempo
- **Best For:** Mobile QBs, fast WRs
- **Bonuses:** +3 Speed (skill positions), +2 Route Running
- **Penalties:** -2 Run Blocking

#### Pro Style
- **Philosophy:** Balanced run/pass attack
- **Best For:** Traditional pocket QBs, power RBs
- **Bonuses:** +2 all offensive attributes (balanced)
- **Penalties:** None (jack of all trades)

#### Air Raid
- **Philosophy:** Pass-heavy, vertical passing
- **Best For:** Strong-armed QBs, deep threat WRs
- **Bonuses:** +4 Deep Accuracy, +3 Deep Route Running
- **Penalties:** -3 Run Blocking, -2 Carrying

#### Power Run
- **Philosophy:** Physical, downhill running
- **Best For:** Power RBs, strong OL
- **Bonuses:** +4 Trucking, +3 Run Blocking, +2 Strength
- **Penalties:** -3 all passing attributes

#### Zone Run
- **Philosophy:** Outside zone, misdirection
- **Best For:** Elusive RBs, athletic OL
- **Bonuses:** +4 Elusiveness, +3 Agility, +2 Vision
- **Penalties:** -2 Trucking

---

### Defensive Schemes (DC)

#### 4-3 Base
- **Philosophy:** Four down linemen, three LBs
- **Best For:** Strong DL, versatile LBs
- **Bonuses:** +3 to DL pass rush, +2 LB tackling
- **Penalties:** -2 DB coverage (fewer DBs on field)

#### 3-4 Base
- **Philosophy:** Three down linemen, four LBs
- **Best For:** Big DL, pass-rushing OLBs
- **Bonuses:** +3 to OLB pass rush, +2 flexibility
- **Penalties:** -2 DL run stopping

#### Cover 2
- **Philosophy:** Two deep safeties, zone coverage
- **Best For:** Fast safeties, zone CBs
- **Bonuses:** +4 Zone Coverage, +3 deep ball defense
- **Penalties:** -3 Man Coverage, vulnerable to intermediate

#### Cover 3
- **Philosophy:** Three deep zones
- **Best For:** Ball-hawking DBs
- **Bonuses:** +3 Zone Coverage, +2 INTs
- **Penalties:** -2 run defense

#### Man Blitz
- **Philosophy:** Aggressive man coverage + pressure
- **Best For:** Lockdown CBs, pass rushers
- **Bonuses:** +5 Man Coverage, +4 pass rush
- **Penalties:** -4 Zone Coverage, vulnerable to big plays

#### Zone Blitz
- **Philosophy:** Disguised blitzes, zone drops
- **Best For:** Smart LBs, versatile DL
- **Bonuses:** +3 Play Recognition, +3 Blitzing
- **Penalties:** -2 Man Coverage

---

### Special Teams Philosophy (ST)

#### Aggressive Returns
- **Philosophy:** Return everything, big play focus
- **Best For:** Elite returners
- **Bonuses:** +5 Return yards, +3% return TD chance
- **Penalties:** +2% turnover risk on returns

#### Conservative Returns
- **Philosophy:** Fair catch often, field position focus
- **Best For:** Ball security, complementary football
- **Bonuses:** -3% turnover risk, better field position
- **Penalties:** -5 return yards per attempt

#### Coverage Specialist
- **Philosophy:** Elite coverage units
- **Best For:** Fast special teamers
- **Bonuses:** -10 opponent return yards
- **Penalties:** Neutral

## SCHEME FIT SYSTEM

### How Scheme Fit Works
- Each player has hidden "scheme fit" rating for each scheme (0-100%)
- Perfect fit: +5 OVR
- Good fit: +2 OVR
- Neutral fit: 0 OVR
- Poor fit: -2 OVR
- Terrible fit: -5 OVR

### Determining Scheme Fit
**Player attributes are compared to scheme requirements:**

Example - West Coast Offense:
- Requires: 85+ Short Accuracy (QB), 80+ Short Route Running (WR)
- Player: QB with 90 Short Accuracy, 70 Deep Accuracy
- Result: Perfect fit (+5 OVR in West Coast)

Example - 3-4 Defense:
- Requires: 280+ lbs DL, 85+ Strength
- Player: 265 lbs DE with 90 Speed
- Result: Poor fit (-2 OVR in 3-4, better for 4-3)

### Changing Schemes
- Can change scheme in offseason
- Players take 4 games to adjust to new scheme
- Some players never fit certain schemes

## COACH CONTRACTS & HIRING

### Contract Structure
- **Length:** 1-5 years
- **Salary:** $1M - $15M per year (affects cap in advanced mode)
- **Performance Bonuses:** Playoffs, division title, championship

### Hiring Process
1. Fire current coach (optional)
2. Browse available coaches
3. View coach ratings, scheme, perks
4. Make offer (salary, years)
5. Coach accepts/rejects based on team situation

### Firing Coaches
- Can fire at any time (pay buyout)
- Buyout = remaining contract value
- Poor performance = automatic firing suggestions
- Player morale drops if popular coach is fired

## COACH DEVELOPMENT & PROGRESSION

### Coach XP System
- Coaches earn XP from wins, achievements
- XP spent to upgrade attributes (like players)
- XP spent to unlock/upgrade perks

### XP Gain
- **Win:** 100 XP
- **Playoff Win:** 250 XP
- **Division Title:** 500 XP
- **Conference Title:** 1,000 XP
- **Championship:** 2,500 XP
- **Coach of the Year:** 1,500 XP

### Attribute Upgrades
- Cost: 500 XP per +1 attribute point
- Max: Can't exceed 99 in any attribute
- Strategic choices (focus on strengths or fix weaknesses)

### Perk Upgrades
- Tier 1: 1,000 XP
- Tier 2: 3,000 XP
- Tier 3: 7,000 XP

### Age & Retirement
- Coaches age each season
- 60-65: Prime years
- 66-70: Experience bonus, but may retire
- 71+: High retirement chance each year
- Can convince to stay with bonuses

## ADVANCED FEATURES

### Coach Mentorship
- Veteran coach can mentor young coach on staff
- Young coach inherits some perks/scheme knowledge
- Succession planning for retirement

### Coach Trees
- Coaches who worked together have connection
- Hiring coaches from same tree = chemistry bonus
- Historical coaching lineages

### Coach Prestige
- Successful coaches gain prestige
- Higher prestige = attract better free agents
- Can poach coaches from other teams

### Dynamic Coach Offers
- Other teams can offer your coordinators HC jobs
- Lose coordinator if you don't promote/pay more
- Must hire replacement mid-season

---

## BALANCE CONSIDERATIONS

### Coach Impact vs Player Talent
- Elite coach with average players: 7-9 wins
- Average coach with elite players: 11-5 wins
- Elite coach with elite players: 13-3+ wins
- Coaches enhance, don't replace talent

### Scheme Fit Importance
- Perfect scheme fit can make 75 OVR play like 80 OVR
- Terrible fit can make 85 OVR play like 80 OVR
- Worth changing schemes to fit personnel

### Perk Balance
- Tier 3 perks should feel transformative
- No single perk makes team unstoppable
- Multiple Tier 3 perks = dynasty potential

### Design Philosophy
- Coaches provide strategic depth without micromanagement
- Scheme fit creates meaningful roster decisions
- Perks offer customization and progression
- Hiring/firing adds drama and consequence

### Realism vs Gameplay
- Coaching changes can swing games (like real NFL)
- Great coaches are difference makers
- Bad coaches hold teams back
- Balanced to keep focus on players
