# Football Franchise Mobile Game - Badges System

## Overview
Badges are **situational bonuses** that activate under specific conditions. Players can earn badges through **XP investment** or by **achieving performance milestones**. Badges provide temporary attribute boosts when their conditions are met.

---

## BADGE TIERS

### Tier Structure
- **Bronze** - Small boost, easier to earn
- **Silver** - Medium boost, moderate difficulty
- **Gold** - Large boost, difficult to earn
- **Hall of Fame (HoF)** - Elite boost, very rare/legendary

### Tier Effects Scale
- **Bronze:** +2 to +3 boost
- **Silver:** +4 to +5 boost
- **Gold:** +6 to +8 boost
- **Hall of Fame:** +9 to +12 boost

---

## BADGE ACQUISITION

### XP-Based Badges (Purchasable)
- Cost XP points to unlock
- Can upgrade through tiers (Bronze ‚Üí Silver ‚Üí Gold ‚Üí HoF)
- Player choice on which badges to invest in
- **XP Costs:**
  - Bronze: 500 XP
  - Silver: 1,500 XP
  - Gold: 3,500 XP
  - Hall of Fame: 8,000 XP

### Performance-Based Badges (Earned)
- Automatically awarded for achievements
- Cannot be purchased with XP
- More prestigious than XP badges
- Example requirements:
  - Make X clutch plays
  - Win X games
  - Achieve specific stat milestones

---

## BADGE LIMITS

### Per Player
- **Minimum:** 0 badges (rookies/low-tier players)
- **Maximum:** 6 badges equipped
- **Average:** 2-3 badges per player

### Stacking Rules
- Multiple badges CAN stack
- All active badges apply simultaneously
- Example: "Clutch" + "Prime Time" + "Playoff Performer" all active if conditions overlap

## BADGE MANAGEMENT

### Equipping Badges
- Open player profile
- Navigate to "Badges" tab
- Drag & drop badges into slots (max 6)
- Can swap badges between games/weeks
- Cannot change during games

### Upgrading Badges
- Spend XP to upgrade tier (Bronze ‚Üí Silver ‚Üí Gold ‚Üí HoF)
- Must own lower tier to upgrade
- Cumulative cost to reach HoF from scratch: 13,500 XP
- Performance-based badges cannot be purchased/upgraded

### Removing Badges
- Can unequip badges at any time
- XP is NOT refunded
- Badge remains in player's collection
- Can re-equip later without cost

## BADGE SYNERGIES

### Stackable Combinations
**Maximum Clutch:**
- Clutch (HoF) + Playoff Performer (HoF) + Comeback Kid (HoF)
- In playoff game, trailing in 4Q, final 2 min = +34 to all attributes

**Red Zone Domination:**
- Red Zone Threat (Gold) + Prime Time (Silver) + Clutch (Gold)
- Prime time game, red zone, final 2 min = +19 combined boost

**Defensive Lockdown:**
- Shutdown Corner (Gold) + Playoff Performer (Silver) + Big Game Player (Gold)
- Playoff game with implications, man coverage = +20 to coverage attributes

## PROGRESSION PATH EXAMPLE

### Example: Rookie QB Development
**Year 1 (Rookie):**
- Earn 2,500 XP through season
- Purchase: Clutch (Bronze) - 500 XP
- Purchase: Red Zone QB (Bronze) - 500 XP
- Remaining: 1,500 XP saved

**Year 2:**
- Earn 3,000 XP
- Available: 4,500 XP
- Upgrade: Clutch (Bronze ‚Üí Silver) - 1,500 XP
- Upgrade: Red Zone QB (Bronze ‚Üí Silver) - 1,500 XP
- Earned: Playoff Performer (Bronze) - first playoff appearance
- Remaining: 1,500 XP

**Year 3-4:**
- Earned: Prime Time (Silver) - 15 prime time games with 85+ grade
- Upgrade: Clutch (Silver ‚Üí Gold) - 3,500 XP
- Upgrade: Red Zone QB (Silver ‚Üí Gold) - 3,500 XP

**Year 5+ (Veteran):**
- Earned: Playoff Performer (HoF) - Super Bowl MVP
- Upgrade: Clutch (Gold ‚Üí HoF) - 8,000 XP
- Full collection of 6 elite badges

## NOTES

### Balance Considerations
- XP should be scarce enough that badge choices matter
- Performance-based badges are more prestigious
- Hall of Fame tier should feel legendary (5% of players)
- Situational nature prevents "always on" power creep

### Design Philosophy
- Badges reward specific playstyles
- Create meaningful build diversity
- Situational activation prevents overpowered stacking
- Mix of earned and purchased creates progression paths

### Future Expansion
- Team chemistry badges (bonus when multiple players have same badge)
- Legacy badges (transferred from retired players)
- Seasonal badges (special limited-time badges)
- Achievement badges for career milestones
