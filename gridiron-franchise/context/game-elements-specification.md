# Football Franchise Mobile Game - Comprehensive Elements List

## 1. PLAYERS
- **Identity:** ID, name, nickname, age, date of birth, nationality, hometown
- **Physical:** height, weight, body type, arm length, hand size
- **Position:** primary position, secondary position, position group
- **Ratings:** overall rating (0-99), potential rating, ceiling/floor
- **Attributes by Position:**
  - QB: throw power, throw accuracy (short/mid/deep), mobility, awareness, decision making
  - RB: speed, acceleration, agility, power, vision, catching, pass blocking
  - WR: speed, route running, catching, catch in traffic, release, jumping
  - TE: blocking, catching, speed, route running
  - OL: pass blocking, run blocking, strength, awareness, footwork
  - DL: pass rush, run defense, strength, finesse moves, power moves
  - LB: coverage, tackling, speed, pursuit, block shedding
  - DB: coverage, man coverage, zone coverage, speed, tackling, ball skills
  - ST: kick power, kick accuracy, punt power, punt accuracy, return ability
- **Skills & Traits:** clutch, injury prone, leader, work ethic, consistency, big game player
- **Development:** age curve, XP, skill progression, regression rate
- **Contract:** salary, years remaining, bonuses, cap hit, trade value
- **Status:** active, injured, suspended, practice squad, free agent
- **Statistics:** career stats, season stats, game stats by category
- **Personal:** morale, loyalty, ego, durability, stamina, energy
- **History:** draft info, college, previous teams, awards, milestones

## 2. COACHES
- **Identity:** ID, name, age, nationality, photo
- **Type:** head coach, offensive coordinator, defensive coordinator, special teams coordinator, position coaches
- **Ratings:** overall rating, scheme fit, player development, game planning, in-game adjustments
- **Specialties:** offense type (run-heavy, pass-heavy, balanced, west coast, spread, etc.)
- **Specialties:** defense type (3-4, 4-3, nickel, zone, man, blitz-heavy)
- **Attributes:** motivation, discipline, play calling, clock management, challenge success
- **Skills:** quarterback whisperer, defensive guru, offensive minded, rebuild specialist
- **Contract:** salary, years remaining, buyout clause
- **History:** coaching tree, previous teams, championships, awards
- **Philosophy:** aggressive/conservative, risk tolerance, 4th down tendency
- **Reputation:** prestige level, recruiting ability, media relations

## 3. TEAMS
- **Identity:** team ID, team name, abbreviation, city, founded year
- **Branding:** primary color, secondary color, logo, uniform variations
- **Stadium:** name, capacity, surface type, weather effects, home field advantage rating
- **Division:** conference, division
- **Record:** wins, losses, ties, win percentage, division standing, conference standing
- **Roster:** 53-man roster, practice squad, injured reserve
- **Depth Chart:** starters by position, backups, formations
- **Playbook:** offensive playbook, defensive playbook, special teams plays
- **Scheme:** offensive scheme, defensive scheme, tempo
- **Team Stats:** points per game, points allowed, yards per game, yards allowed, turnover differential
- **Team Ratings:** offense rating, defense rating, special teams rating, overall team rating
- **Chemistry:** team morale, locker room cohesion, winning culture
- **Finances:** salary cap, cap space, team value, revenue
- **History:** championships, playoff appearances, retired numbers, hall of famers
- **Rival Teams:** division rivals, historical rivals
- **Draft Assets:** current year picks, future picks, compensatory picks

## 4. STAFF
- **Front Office:**
  - General Manager: personnel rating, contract negotiation, draft skill, talent evaluation
  - Owner: wealth, patience, win-now vs rebuild preference, spending willingness
  - Director of Player Personnel
  - Scouts: regional scouts, area scouts, pro scouts, college scouts
- **Medical Staff:**
  - Head Team Doctor: injury diagnosis accuracy, recovery time reduction
  - Athletic Trainers: conditioning impact, injury prevention
  - Sports Psychologist: morale management, mental health
- **Support Staff:**
  - Strength & Conditioning Coach: player development in physical attributes
  - Nutritionist: stamina and recovery optimization
  - Video Coordinator: game planning bonus
  - Equipment Manager: uniform and equipment quality
- **Staff Ratings:** each staff member has competency rating (0-99)
- **Staff Budget:** allocation for hiring quality staff

## 5. GAMES
- **Game Identity:** game ID, week number, season, date, time
- **Matchup:** home team, away team, location, weather conditions
- **Score:** quarter-by-quarter scoring, final score, overtime
- **Game Stats:**
  - Team stats: total yards, passing yards, rushing yards, turnovers, penalties, time of possession
  - Player stats: individual performance by position
  - Play-by-play log
  - Key moments: big plays, scoring drives, turnovers, injuries
- **Game Context:** playoff game, division game, prime time, rivalry
- **Broadcast:** TV network, announcers, viewership
- **Betting:** spread, over/under, moneyline, actual results
- **Weather:** temperature, precipitation, wind speed, field conditions
- **Officials:** referee crew, penalty calls, challenge outcomes
- **Injuries:** in-game injuries, severity, return timeline
- **Milestones:** player achievements during game, records broken
- **Game Narrative:** key storylines, turning points, star performers
- **Attendance:** tickets sold, stadium atmosphere

## 6. SEASONS
- **Season Identity:** season year, league year
- **Season Type:** preseason, regular season, postseason
- **Schedule:** 
  - Week structure (typically 18 weeks regular season)

  - Division games, conference games, inter-conference games
  - Prime time slots
- **Standings:** division standings, conference standings, playoff picture
- **Playoffs:**
  - Wild card round
  - Divisional round
  - Conference championships
  - Super Bowl
  - Playoff seeding rules
- **Season Awards:**
  - MVP, Offensive/Defensive Player of the Year
  - Rookie of the Year, Coach of the Year
  - All-Pro teams, Pro Bowl selections
  - Comeback Player of the Year
- **Draft:** 7 rounds, draft order, compensatory picks
- **Free Agency:** signing period, franchise tags, transition tags
- **Trade Deadline:** midseason trading window
- **Season Stats Leaders:** passing, rushing, receiving, defensive categories
- **Team Achievements:** playoff berths, division titles, championships

## 13. ATTRIBUTES BREAKDOWN
### Offensive Attributes
- **Quarterback:** throw power, short accuracy, medium accuracy, deep accuracy, play action, throw on run, throw under pressure, awareness, break sacks
- **Running Back:** speed, acceleration, agility, carrying, break tackle, trucking, elusiveness, juke, spin, stiff arm, catching, pass blocking
- **Wide Receiver:** speed, acceleration, catching, catch in traffic, spectacular catch, release, route running (short/medium/deep), jumping, run after catch
- **Tight End:** catching, run blocking, pass blocking, speed, route running, release, catch in traffic
- **Offensive Line:** pass blocking, run blocking, pass block power, pass block finesse, run block power, run block finesse, impact blocking, awareness, strength

### Defensive Attributes
- **Defensive Line:** power moves, finesse moves, block shedding, strength, pursuit, play recognition, tackling
- **Linebacker:** speed, acceleration, pursuit, play recognition, zone coverage, man coverage, tackling, hit power, block shedding, jumping
- **Defensive Back:** man coverage, zone coverage, press, speed, acceleration, agility, jumping, play recognition, catching, tackling

### Special Teams Attributes
- **Kicker:** kick power, kick accuracy
- **Punter:** punt power, punt accuracy
- **Returner:** return ability, speed, elusiveness, catching

### Universal Attributes
- **Physical:** speed, acceleration, agility, jumping, strength, stamina, injury rating
- **Mental:** awareness, play recognition, football IQ
- **Intangibles:** clutch, consistency, toughness, penalty tendency
